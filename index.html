About Me

Hello world! My name is Austin Kang and I am a 22 year old student currently enrolled at UCI's Coding Bootcamp. For the past few years I have been searching for a career path that fits my best interests and recently a friend of mine introduced me to coding. It really caught my attention so I started going online and tried to learn it myself, but was only able to get so far. That's when I found out about the coding bootcamp and decided I wanted to enroll immediately. I'm hoping that after this 6 month intensive my skills in coding have improved tremendously. My goal is to land a job at a company as a web-developer. I'm super excited to be here and motivated to learn!
